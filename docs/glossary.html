Glossary

Plain-language definitions for the public v1.0 static release. CSV and SQLite downloads retain internal schema fields for ML and reproducibility.

Terms

ItemDetails
Methanol-tolerance scoreReference-condition score for methanol tolerance. lower = more tolerant; release values are frozen for reproducibility.
ConfidenceHow much support the score has after checking baseline activity, missing assay context, source traceability, and sensitivity to evidence choices.
Methanol tolerance badgePublic badge shown as More tolerant, Uncertain, or Less tolerant. It summarizes stable rank behavior and does not replace the numeric score.
Fit statusWhether the score is directly supported, supported by limited evidence, insufficiently supported, or not applicable.
Enzyme groupReadable parent, duplicate, or benchmark grouping used for browsing. It is provisional and not a final taxonomy.
evidence tierEvidence strength tier: strong, moderate, or limited support after assay and provenance checks.
source typeWhether support comes from literature, patent evidence, both, or no linked methanol-tolerance evidence.
assay method classBroad measurement-readout group, such as spectrophotometric/colorimetric, fluorometric, titrimetric/pH-stat, or biodiesel-conversion assays.
substrate classBroad substrate group used to make condition filters easier to scan before choosing exact substrates.
SEQ ID NO专利序列表中某条序列的编号（Sequence Listing ID），用于指向专利中一条具体蛋白或核酸序列。
family tolerance summaryA group-level overview that helps users inspect trends; it is not a substitute for construct-level evidence.
95% intervalUncertainty interval around the score. It communicates uncertainty; it is not itself the public tolerance badge.

lower = more tolerant. A lower LTS_ref means the enzyme or construct is predicted to carry less methanol-induced inactive burden under the reference challenge.

Public pages intentionally avoid internal primary keys and workflow fields in table headers. Use the downloadable CSV/SQLite bundle when exact IDs, protocol keys, or provenance fields are required for computation.
