Terms

FieldValue
LTS_refReference-condition latent tolerance score. lower = more tolerant; values are frozen Phase 1.5 outputs.
confidence_LTSConfidence for the LTS_ref estimate after considering A0 quality, missing assay context, provenance, and sensitivity stability.
tolerance_tierPublic methanol-tolerance badge: More tolerant, Uncertain, or Less tolerant. It is based on rank stability, not a hard CI class.
fit_statusWhether the LTS estimate was directly fitted, partially pooled because of low evidence, insufficiently supported, or not applicable.
scaffold_familyStructural-family or scaffold proxy used for grouping entries and later structure-module analysis.
evidence tierAssay/provenance quality tier: core training, downweighted training, or holdout-only evidence.
source typeWhether support comes from literature, patent evidence, both, or no linked methanol-tolerance evidence.
assay method classBroad measurement-readout group, such as spectrophotometric/colorimetric, fluorometric, titrimetric/pH-stat, or biodiesel-conversion assays.
substrate classBroad substrate group used to make condition filters easier to scan before choosing exact substrates.
family tolerance summaryA structural-family level overview that helps users inspect family trends; it is not a substitute for construct-level LTS_ref.
95% CIModel interval around LTS_ref. It communicates uncertainty; it is not itself the public tolerance tier.

lower = more tolerant. A lower LTS_ref means the enzyme or construct is predicted to carry less methanol-induced inactive burden under the reference challenge.

Public pages intentionally avoid internal primary keys and workflow fields in table headers. Use the downloadable CSV/SQLite bundle when exact IDs, protocol keys, or provenance fields are required for computation.
