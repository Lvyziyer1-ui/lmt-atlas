Known Limitations

Known Limitations

Use these constraints when interpreting methanol-tolerance scores, evidence tables, and raw residual activity values.

  • Measurement rows are individual data points, not independent samples. The release contains 2740 measurement rows, but the construct-level modeling layer is 440 construct units from 163 sources.
  • Most construct-level records have uncertain confidence. Among the 440 construct units, many entries remain uncertain or intermediate because source evidence is sparse, heterogeneous, or sensitivity-dependent.
  • LipA9 is an incremental v2.0 entry with low evidence support, a wide interval, and digitized Fig. 4b values that need review. It is included in the 440-construct layer as an Uncertain construct and should not be used as high-confidence validation until Fig. 4b is redigitized.
  • Incubation temperature is missing from 28.96% of raw direct rows. Missing temperatures are disclosed as missing and are not replaced with room temperature, optimum temperature, or model estimates.
  • Residual activity values should not be compared directly across unmatched temperatures, methods, methanol concentrations, or exposure times. Filter to comparable assay conditions before interpreting raw activity differences.
